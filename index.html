ABOUT ME

I graduated from CUNY Baruch College in 2011 with a BA in Political Science and Sociology, with a focus on International Affairs.

During college, I interned at nonprofits in NYC and New Delhi and forged a commitment to philanthropy and working in civil society. Since then, I've continued my career in nonprofits by working at the Open Society Foundations as a Program Specialist, developing international scholarship programs for human rights activists from closed countries. More recently, my focus has been closer to home, working at All Star Code as a Program Manager where I manage a Summer Intensive program for young men of color to learn computer science and entrepreneurship skills. In my role, I focus on program design, expansion and monitoring and evaluation.

In my free time, I've participated in opportunities to continue learning through advisory groups and consulting. For fun, I run a book club that's been meeting in NYC Thai restaurants since 2011.

Reach out to me if you have any career or consulting opportunities, or recommendations for Thai restaurants.
